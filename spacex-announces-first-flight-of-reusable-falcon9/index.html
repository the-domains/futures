SpaceX announces first flight of Reusable Falcon9

SES and SpaceX announced today they have reached an agreement to launch SES-10 on a re-used Falcon 9 rocket booster.

Having been the first commercial satellite operator to launch with SpaceX back in 2013, we are excited to once again be the first customer to launch on SpaceX's first ever mission using a flight-proven rocket.
